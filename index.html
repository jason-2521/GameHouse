Leap Void

Master the art of survival in this neon-lit cyberpunk platformer

Game Overview

Leap Void is an intense platformer that tests your reflexes and timing. Navigate through a futuristic world filled with neon obstacles and deadly traps. Your mission is simple: survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and achieving high scores.

How to Play

  • • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your character
  • • Time your jumps perfectly to avoid obstacles
  • • Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities
  • • Survive as long as possible to achieve high scores

Features

  • • Stunning cyberpunk visuals
  • • Progressive difficulty system
  • • Power-up system
  • • Global leaderboards
  • • Responsive controls

Tips & Tricks

  • • Watch for pattern changes in obstacles
  • • Don't get greedy with power-ups
  • • Practice timing your jumps
  • • Stay focused on upcoming obstacles
